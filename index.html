About Me

I am Shruti Gaonkar. I live in Los Angeles, California. I am originally from Goa, a small state in India known mainly for its beaches, architectural monuments, and churches.

As a college graduate in Computer Engineering, I was confused as to what would be the best career option in this field given that it is so vast. Dropping my resume in and around the city, I met my first employer through an acquaintance. He had a start-up and was looking for freshers. That is where I landed my first job in web development. I was mainly involved in the backend development but also got to work on frontend over a while.

I am Certified in UCLA Full Stack Web Development Program. I am ready to expand my career with these new skills.
